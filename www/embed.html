A License to Blight

Tax delinquent landlords and speculators are the biggest source of blight in Philadelphia, racking up code violations at a far higher rate than tax-compliant property owners. The correlation between tax delinquency and irresponsible property ownership is particularly pronounced in low-income neighborhoods.

For each census tract, the chart below links delinquency data with city violations issued over the past year for a number of key quality of life categories, including property maintenance, vacant land and city-funded demolitions of dangerous properties. In each category, the correlation between blight and investor-owned delinquent property is strong.
